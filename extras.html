Anatomika — Exhibition Support

Information about the upcoming exhibition and how to support it.

About the Exhibition

I'm co-curating an exhibition called Anatomika that explores speculative body systems and experimental bio-aesthetics. The show will include new work, workshops, and community programming. Dates, venue, and schedule will be updated here as the project develops.

How your support helps

Donations will be used to cover venue rental, materials for participants, artist honoraria, and accessibility services (e.g., live captioning, large-print materials).
